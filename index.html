Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Are you redesigning our website?

No. This focuses on technical health, structure, and performance—not visual redesign.

Why don't ads start immediately?

Launching ads before fixing crawl access, tracking, and structure leads to wasted spend. Month 1 prevents that.

What if ads don't perform?

Performance is reviewed monthly. Budgets can be adjusted or paused based on results.

How long before we see traction?

Foundational improvements occur in Month 1. Ads typically stabilize between Months 3–5.

Is this a long-term contract?

Initial term is 6 months. Continuation is optional and month-to-month.

Who pays Google Ads costs?

Ad spend is paid directly to Google. Poppy manages strategy and optimization.
